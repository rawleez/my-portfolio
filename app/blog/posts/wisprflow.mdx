# Feature Idea: Context-Aware Quote Capture

### Problem

People use Wispr Flow to capture their own thoughts, but also want to quickly save things other people say from YouTube, meetings, podcasts, or lectures. Manual attribution is tedious and quotes lose context.

### Customer

* Students
* Knowledge workers
* Researchers
* Anyone learning from spoken content

### Solution

A "Quote Capture" mode that automatically attributes captured speech with:

* Speaker
* Source
* Timestamp
* Link (when available)

Instead of raw transcripts, optionally organize content into:

* Key insights
* Quotes
* FAQs
* Action items

### PM Considerations

* Validate top use cases (YouTube vs. meetings vs. lectures)
* Start with browser media where attribution is reliable
* Address recording consent, privacy, and legal requirements
* Determine whether this belongs in Flow or as a dedicated capture product

**Core insight:** Don't just transcribe speech—turn it into structured, attributable knowledge.
